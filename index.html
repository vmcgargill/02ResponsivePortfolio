About Me

Hello, my name is Gaius Julius Caesar. I was once Consul of Rome, and I helped shape the Roman Republic into a Roman Empire. I am one of the most famous historical figures to ever walk the face of earth.

My Life

I was born on July 12th, 100 BCE and was assasinated on March 15th, 44 BCE. During my tenure, I conqured Gaul and helped deafeat Sparticus during a rebellion. My legacy as the founder of the Roman Empire lives on more than 2000 years later.
